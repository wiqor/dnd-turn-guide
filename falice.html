[A] Cast a Spell

  • Your Spellcasting Ability is Charisma.
  • Your Spell Save DC is 15. (8 + CHA + PROF)
  • Your Spell Attack Bonus is +7. (CHA + PROF)

[A] Countercharm

  • Bard Feature
  • You start a performance that lasts until the end of your next turn.
  • During that time, you and any allies within 30ft gain Advantage on Saving Throws against Frightened or Charmed.
  • Creatures must be able to hear you to gain this benefit.
  • The performance ends early if you are Incapacitated, Silenced, or choose to end it (no action required).

[A] Disengage

  • If you take the Disengage action, your movement doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of the turn.

[A] Dodge

  • When you take the Dodge action, you focus entirely on avoiding attacks. Until the start of your next turn, any attack roll made against you has disadvantage if you can see the attacker, and you make Dexterity saving throws with advantage.

[A] Dash

  • When you take the Dash Action, your movement speed is doubled for the rest of the turn.

[A] Hide

  • When you take the Hide action, you make a Stealth check in an attempt to hide, following the rules for hiding. If you succeed, you gain certain benefits, as described in the “Unseen Attackers and Targets” section.

[A] Help

  • When you take the Help action, the creature you aid gains advantage on the next ability check it makes to perform the task you are helping with, provided that it makes the check before the start of your next turn.
  • Alternatively, you can aid a friendly creature in attacking a creature within 5 feet of you. You feint, distract the target, or in some other way team up to make your ally’s attack more effective. If your ally attacks the target before your next turn, the first attack roll is made with advantage.

[A] Ready

  • The Ready Action allows you to use an Action as a Reaction in response to a trigger.
  • First, define the trigger. (Example, "If that goblin moves close to me,")
  • Then, define the Action you'll take in response. (Example, "then I'll Attack him.")
  • You take this Reaction before the start of your next turn. If the trigger happens, you can choose to ignore it.

[A] Use an Item

  • You normally interact with an object while doing something else, such as when you draw a sword as part of an attack. When an object requires your action for its use, you take the Use an Object action. This action is also useful when you want to interact with more than one object on your turn.

[B] Bonus Action

⤷ [B] Dual Wielding

  • When you Attack Action and Attack with a Light Melee Weapon, use a Bonus Action to Attack with a different Light Melee Weapon in your offhand.
  • You don't add your Ability Modifier to the Damage of this attack.

⤷ [B] Drink a Potion

  • Drink a Potion. Yum.

⤷ [B] Bardic Inspiration

  • Bard Feature
  • Choose one creature other than yourself within 60ft that can hear you.
  • That creature gains an Inspiration Die (1d8).
  • Once within 10 minutes, the creature can add the Inspiration Die to an Ability Check, Attack Roll, or Saving Throw. It is then lost.
  • Magical Inspiration: If a creature has an Inspiration Die and casts a Spell that restores Hit Points or deals Damage, they can add the Inspiration Die to the Hit Points restored or the Damage dealt. It is then lost.
  • You have 4 Uses. Regain all on a Long Rest.
  • Font of Inspiration: You regain all of your uses of Bardic Inspiration when you finish a Short or Long Rest.
  • Uses:

⤷ [B] Mantle of Inspiration

  • Bard Feature
  • Expend a use of Bardic Inspiration to choose a number of creatures you can see and can see you within 60ft equal to your CHA (4).
  • They gain 8 Temporary Hit Points and can immediately use their Reaction to move up to their Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

⤷ [B] Mantle of Majesty

  • Eladrin Racial Feature
  • You cast Command without expending a spell slot.
  • You take on the appearance of unearthly beauty for 1 minute or until your Concentration ends.
  • During this time, you can cast Command as a Bonus Action on each of your turns without expending a spell slot.
  • A creature charmed by you automatically fail its Saving Throw against the Command spell you cast with this feature.
  • You have 1 Use. Regain all on a Long Rest.
  • Uses:

⤷ [B] Fey Step

  • Eladrin Racial Feature
  • Teleport up to 30ft to a space you can see.
  • This feature has an additional effect based on your Racial Season.
  • Some of these effects require a Saving Throw.
  • Fey Step Save DC = 15 (8 + PROF + CHA)
  • Spring: When you Fey Step, touch one willing creature within 5ft to teleport them instead of yourself.
  • Summer: Immediately after you Fey Step, creatures within 5ft of you take 3 Fire Damage.
  • Autumn: Immediately after you Fey Step, up to two creatures of your choice within 10ft of you make a WIS Save. On failure, they are Charmed by you for 1 minute or until they take damage.
  • Winter: When you Fey Step, one creature of your choice within 5ft of you before you teleport makes a WIS Save. On failure, the creature is Frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

[U] Resources/Uses

[U] Song of Rest

  • Bard Feature
  • You and allies that can hear you restore an additional 1d6 Hit Points after spending any number of Hit Dice at the end of a Short Rest.

[U] Enthralling Performance

  • Bard Feature
  • Perform for at least 1 minute to choose up to 4 creatures within 60ft that watched the performance.
  • Those creatures make a WIS Save against your Spell Save DC. On failure, they are Charmed by you for 1 hour, until they take damage, or if you Attack them or any of their allies.
  • While Charmed in this way, they idolize you, speak glowingly of you, and hinders anyone who opposes you; avoiding violence unless it was already inclined to fight for you.
  • On success, the creatures don't know you tried to charm them.
  • You have 1 Use. Regain all on a Short/Long Rest.
  • Uses:

[R] Reaction

⤷ [R] Opportunity Attack

  • You can make an opportunity attack when a hostile creature that you can see moves out of your reach.
  •  You can use your reaction to make 1 Melee Attack against the provoking creature. The attack occurs right before the creature leaves your reach.

[F] Free Action

⤷ [F] Object Interaction

  • You can interact with one object during your turn.
  • Opening a door, swapping to a shield or weapon, or anything like that.

⤷ [F] Ability Check

  • You can make an Ability Check during your turn, except for the following:
  • Investigation Checks require using the Search Action to find something.
  • Stealth Checks require using the Hide Action to conceal yourself.
  • Medicine Checks require an Action to stabilize a friend.

[M] Movement

⤷ [M] Move

  • You can Move up to your Speed during your turn.
  • You can Dash Action to double your Speed for the turn.
  • If you have multiple Speeds, such as a Flying speed, you can use both in combination. When you switch to a different Speed, subtract the distance you've already Moved from that Speed.
  • You can break up your Movement between other actions on your turn.

⤷ [M] Climbing

  • You can Climb using your Speed. Climbing 1ft costs 2ft of Movement.

⤷ [M] Swimming

  • You can Swim using your Speed. Swimming 1ft costs 2ft of Movement.

⤷ [M] Jumping

  • Long Jump: You can jump forward a number of feet equal to your STR Score if you Move at least 10ft on foot immediately before the Jump. If you don't, you can only jump half that distance.
  • High Jump: You can jump upward a number of feet equal to 3 + your STR Modifier if you Move at least 10ft on foot immediately before the Jump. If you don't, you can only jump half that distance.
  • Either way, each 1ft of the Jump costs 1ft of Movement.

Passive Effects/Bonuses

  • Darkvision: You can see in Dim Light to range of 60ft as if it were Bright Light, and Darkness as if it were Dim Light. You discern colors in that Darkness only as shades of grey.
  • Font of Inspiration: You regain all of your uses of Bardic Inspiration when you finish a Short or Long Rest.

⤷ Ability Checks

  • Expertise: Two of your Skill Proficiencies are doubled when you use them.
  • Jack of All Trades: Add half your PROF, rounded down, to any Ability Check that doesn't already use your PROF.

⤷ Saving Throws

  • Fey Ancestry: You have Advantage on Saving Throws made against or to end Charmed.
